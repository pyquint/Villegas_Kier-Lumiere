Subtle motion and atmosphere layers that elevate the experience without overwhelming it.

07

Do's & Don'ts

Guidelines for maintaining visual consistency when extending or modifying the design.

✓ Do

Use Playfair Display for all headings, DM Sans for all body text.

Keep border-radius at 2–4px for the sharp, editorial feel.

Use gold (#C9A96E) sparingly — kickers, icons, hover states, and accents only.

Maintain generous section padding (6.5rem desktop, 4.5rem mobile).

Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for small labels and CTAs.

✗ Don't

Use rounded corners above 6px — it conflicts with the editorial tone.

Introduce bright or saturated colors beyond the defined palette.

Use more than two font families; the pair is intentionally minimal.

Apply gold as a background fill for large areas — it's an accent, not a surface.

Add heavy drop shadows; prefer subtle border + translateY on hover.
